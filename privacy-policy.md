# NSFWipe Privacy Policy

NSFWipe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What We Access:

- NSFWipe accesses your browser's history, active tab URL, and navigation events for the sole purpose of identifying and removing potentially NSFW entries.

## What We Do NOT Do:

- We do not track or monitor user behavior.
- We do not send any data to external servers.
- We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
- We do not use or sell user data for advertising or analytics.

All data processing happens locally in your browser.

If you have any questions, feel free to open an issue at:
