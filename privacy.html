Safe History Manager - Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Use

Safe History Manager is designed with your privacy in mind. The extension:

  • Does not collect or transmit any personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history outside of its core functionality
  • Stores all data locally on your device
  • Does not use any external servers or services

Local Storage

The extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • URLs of blocked sites (for history management)
  • Custom blocking patterns you create
  • Deletion logs (maintained for transparency)

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time using the extension's interface.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • history: To manage and clean browsing history
  • storage: To save your settings and blocked sites list locally
  • tabs: To manage history cleaning across tabs
  • webNavigation: To detect and handle navigation events

Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear All History" button in the extension
  • Removing the extension from your browser
